New order received!

Urgent. Fragile.

Subject: squirrel. Species: unknown. Status: missing.

The client was vague. All they said was:

“She escaped again. You know the way. Return her to Dema.”

No coordinates. No signal. No name.

Just a trace of fur and the feeling that it was close.

The route is yours to find.

Deliver the squirrel. Deliver hope.
